ABOUT THE FOUNDER
My name is Rani Abraham I got into this business following in my fathers footsteps, he was a contractor who built, remodeled, and designed custom homes for over 20 years. He taught me many things which I carried into my business. I’ve been running my own business since 2015 and worked for him and a few other contractors prior to that. I strive to offer high quality customer service by providing as much info as possible about the projects pros and cons ahead of time, I also ensure the quality of the work done is to meet the expectations of the customer or exceed them. ABOUT US
Born into a family of contractors, building is in our blood. After noticing a lack of skilled professionals in the building and consulting industries, we decided to take matters into our own hands and opened our own company.

At Abraham Building & Consulting, our goal is to take the “con” out of construction. With over 20 years of personal experience and a family with a lifetime of experience, the team at Abraham Building & Consulting can guarantee that we are qualified to handle anything you throw our way.

We do everything from basic remodeling projects to new home constructions, but our specialties are bathrooms and kitchens. Between our group of consultants, contractors, and design team, we can guarantee you are in the best hands!
